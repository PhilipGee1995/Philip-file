Nigeria Defeats Seychelles by 3 goals
in last Afcon Game.



Nigeria defeated Seychelles 3-1 at the Stephen Keshi Stadium on Friday to end

their 2019 Africa Cup of Nations qualifiers on a high.

Odion Ighalo opened the scoring from the penalty spot, before Rody Melanie

- against all odds and all thanks to a Francis Uzoho howler - levelled for the

Pirates to end the first half in a stalemate. Goals from Henry Onyekuru and

substitute Moses Simon won the game for the Super Eagles, who

were unconvincing at times in the dead rubber game.

EURO 2020 LIVE



The Euro 2020 qualifiers sprang up a lot of surprises, with big names getting wins

succesfully. France, Spain, Germany and England all got comfortable wins to boost

their chances of qualifying for the next round.

Ireland, Scotland and Italy also got wins to boost their chances. Debut players also shown

on the big stage, with Italy's Moise Kean getting a debut goal, and Jadon Sancho getting 2

assists for England.
